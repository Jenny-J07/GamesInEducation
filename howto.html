Select Games

When selecting games, it is best to take both age and difficulty level into account. Make sure to check the age to make sure it is appropriate for your class. You should also consider the difficulty level to make sure it won't be too easy or difficult for the children. This could lead to them being disinterested.

Considering Accessibility Needs

Accessibility needs must be considered when selecting games.

Analysing and Testing Games

Testing games is a good way to gauge whether it is appropriate for your class.
