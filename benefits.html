Team Working

By utilising games in your lessons, this can greatly improve children's teamworking skills. There are a range of games that allow for children to work in groups or pairs to complete tasks.

Problem Solving

Games are also a great resourse to improve problem solving skills. Education games can provide challenges for children to solve. This helps to build up their problem solving skills in a fun manner.
