Welcome to Games In Education!

Looking for a new method of teaching, or hoping to make learning more interesting for your students? Then look no further as educational games are the answer! There are a wide range of benefits to using educational games alongside your teaching and this website helps to showcase those alongside giving you the tips and tools you need to get started.

Take a look at the Benefits page to find out how using educational games can have a positive impact on your students. You can also find examples of educational games on our Games page and more details on how to implement educational games on the How to page.



TEAMWORK

Inspire teamwork by having children work together when playing games. This can help build up communication skills as well as teaching children about compromise when disagreeing with teammates.

DECISION MAKING

Improve decision making with games that challenge critical thinking. By deploying games that offer different decisions, it teaches children in a safe game environment that it is okay to make mistakes.

PROBLEM SOLVING

Games offer a fun way for children to improve their problem solving skills. Through interesting puzzles, children can think through problems but remain engaged!
